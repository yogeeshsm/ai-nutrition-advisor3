About AI Nutrition Advisor

Empowering Anganwadi workers with AI-powered meal planning

Project Overview

The AI Nutrition Advisor is a comprehensive meal planning tool designed specifically for Anganwadi workers to generate balanced, nutritious weekly meal plans for children.

Using advanced AI optimization algorithms and ICMR nutritional guidelines, this tool helps create cost-effective meal plans that meet the nutritional requirements of children in different age groups.

Key Features
AI-Powered Optimization

Linear programming algorithm optimizes meal plans to maximize nutrition within budget constraints.

31 Indian Ingredients

Comprehensive database of locally available ingredients with accurate nutritional values.

Visual Analytics

Interactive charts and graphs to understand nutritional composition and track effectiveness.

Multiple Export Formats

Export meal plans as PDF, CSV, or JSON for easy sharing and record-keeping.

Budget Management

Set weekly budgets and get optimal meal plans that stay within financial constraints.

Age Group Specific

Customized nutritional requirements based on ICMR guidelines for different age groups.

Nutrition Scoring

Every meal plan gets a comprehensive nutrition score to ensure quality standards.

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
